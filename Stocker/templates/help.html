How do I buy stocks? ‚ñº

Navigate to the Trade page, select a stock from the dropdown, enter the quantity you want to buy, select "Buy" as the trade type, and click "Execute Trade".

How are stock prices updated? ‚ñº

Stock prices are updated every 10 seconds with simulated market data to provide a realistic trading experience.

Can I sell partial quantities? ‚ñº

Yes, you can sell any quantity up to the total number of shares you own in that stock.

How is my portfolio value calculated? ‚ñº

Portfolio value is calculated using current market prices multiplied by your holdings. P&L shows the difference between your purchase price and current market value.

Where can I view my trading history? ‚ñº

Visit the History page to see all your past trades, including buy and sell orders with timestamps and prices.

Yes, all trading data is securely stored and encrypted. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.
